About Us

Where Craftsmanship
Meets Modern Living

GTIM GROUP is Israel's premier destination for luxury furniture and interior design — a brand built on the belief that every space deserves timeless elegance. Rooted in modern design thinking and shaped by a deep respect for materials, comfort, and durability, we serve discerning private clients, architects, and interior designers across Israel. Each piece in our collection reflects a philosophy of refined living: purposeful, enduring, and unmistakably sophisticated.

Our Story

Crafted for Those Who Refuse to Compromise

GTIM GROUP was founded on a single conviction: that the Israeli market deserves furniture of the same calibre found in the world's most celebrated residences and commercial spaces. We set out to bridge the gap — bringing global-quality craftsmanship, considered design, and uncompromising materials to discerning clients across Israel.

"We do not follow trends. We build pieces that outlast them."

Every collection we curate is selected for its timeless character rather than seasonal appeal. We partner with master craftspeople and innovative ateliers whose philosophy mirrors our own — that a great piece of furniture is not merely functional, but a statement of identity and a legacy of quality. From the living room to the boardroom, each GTIM GROUP piece is chosen to endure.

We serve private clients seeking to elevate their homes, interior designers and architects who demand reliable excellence, and businesses that understand the language of luxury furniture in Israel. Whatever the space, we bring the same depth of expertise, the same curatorial eye, and the same commitment to an exceptional experience — from first consultation to final installation.

Our Foundation

The Pillars Behind Every Piece

01

Craftsmanship

Every piece of luxury furniture in Israel bearing the GTIM GROUP name is the product of exacting precision. Our artisans combine time-honoured joinery techniques with modern manufacturing standards — selecting only the finest solid woods, hand-stitched leathers, and engineered alloys. Tolerances are measured in millimetres. The result is furniture that holds its integrity, its beauty, and its promise across decades of daily life.

02

Innovation

Forward-thinking design is not a trend at GTIM GROUP — it is a discipline. Our design team continuously explores emerging materials, structural engineering advances, and evolving lifestyle needs to create pieces that feel both timeless and contemporary. From modular configurations that adapt to any space to sustainable material sourcing, we redefine what high-end furniture in Israel can be for architects and interior designers who demand the extraordinary.

03

Customer Experience

The GTIM GROUP experience begins the moment you make contact and continues long after delivery. Our dedicated consultants guide private clients, architects, and businesses through every decision — from initial concept and material selection to bespoke customisation and white-glove installation. We believe that attentive, unhurried service is itself a form of craftsmanship, and we hold it to the same uncompromising standard as the furniture we create.
