Private Residence
Living Area—Tel Aviv, Israel

A Residence Redefined in Warmth & Form

Commissioned for a discerning private client in Tel Aviv, this living area project centered on creating a sanctuary of calm sophistication. Our design team curated a bespoke sofa in natural bouclé, complemented by a hand-finished walnut console and custom shelving that frames the room's architectural proportions.

The result is a space that embodies timeless luxury furniture in Israel — where every piece earns its place through material integrity and considered scale.

Commercial
Meeting Lounge—Herzliya, Israel

Executive Presence for a Boutique Office

A leading boutique investment firm sought a meeting lounge that would communicate authority without excess. GTIM GROUP delivered a custom dark-oak conference table paired with hand-stitched charcoal leather seating — each chair engineered for long sessions without sacrificing silhouette.

Integrated shelving in blackened steel and brass-tipped joinery details complete a room that speaks fluently to high-end furniture in Israel's corporate landscape.

03
Designer Suite
Bedroom Suite—Jerusalem, Israel

A Bedroom Suite as Personal Gallery

Developed in close collaboration with a leading Israeli interior designer, this bedroom suite was conceived as a private retreat where every surface rewards attention. The centrepiece — an upholstered king bed in ivory linen — anchors a composition of floating smoked-oak nightstands and a custom wardrobe with fluted glass inserts.

Lighting was treated as furniture: each fixture chosen to graze textures and define depth, turning a bedroom into an experience of layered calm and considered luxury.

  • MaterialsIvory Linen, Smoked Oak, Fluted Glass, Brass
  • OutcomeFull suite design, 6 bespoke custom furniture pieces
